Getting Started

Welcome to the documentation for Elipptic5G's 3D modeling and mapping software designed to transform images captured by drones into immersive and accurate representations of the world around us. Whether you're a seasoned professional in the field of surveying, urban planning, agriculture, or a curious enthusiast exploring the possibilities of drone technology, this documentation is your comprehensive guide to unlocking the full potential of our powerful software.

Quick Start

This guide is designed to get you up and running swiftly, enabling you to harness the power of our tool for transforming drone-captured images into immersive 3D models and maps.

Logging In

Visit threecell.ddns.net and enter the site using the provided test username and password for access.

Create New Project

Click on "New Project," enter the desired name and description, then click "Accept" to create it.

Data Preperation

You need at least 5 images, but 16-32 is typically the minimum

Images must be overlap by %65 or more. Aim for 70-72%

For great 3D, images must overlap by 83%

Processing Images

Go to "Select Images and GCP" and make sure to choose all your images for processing.

Additional Options for Processing

Explore various parameters within the "Options" menu to tailor the settings for specific processes.

Choose "Fast Orthophoto" to expedite processing time and generate solely an orthophoto.

Opt for "3D Model" to concentrate on the 3D modeling process.

Alternatively, stick with the "Default" parameters to experience the full potential of the Elipptic Engine.

Output Options

Expand the "Download Assets" menu to access a variety of formats for downloading. Additionally, if you want to export everything, you can click "All Assets" to download everything in one go.
